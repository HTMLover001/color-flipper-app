FLIGHT ENGINEER

ANOUSHEH ANSARI

ANOUSHEH ANSARI is an Iranian American engineer
and co- founder of Prodea Systems. Ansari was the
fourth self-funded space tourist, the first self-funded
woman to fly to the ISS, and the first
Iranian in space.
